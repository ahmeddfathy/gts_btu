Our Platform

Graduate Tracking System

A comprehensive solution that connects graduates, companies, and the university

Bridging the Gap Between Education and Industry

BSU Tech is a state-of-the-art Graduate Tracking System designed specifically for Beni Suef Technological University. Our platform serves as a comprehensive ecosystem that connects talented graduates with leading companies in technology and engineering fields.

Through our system, the university can effectively monitor graduate career progress, companies can discover exceptional talent, and graduates can access exclusive job opportunities and training programs tailored to their specializations.

Our mission is to enhance graduate employability, strengthen industry-academia relationships, and contribute to the technological advancement of Egypt by ensuring that the skills acquired at the university translate directly into successful careers.

Process

How It Works

Simple steps to get started with BSU Tech Graduate Tracking System

1

Registration

Graduates create a detailed profile, companies register their organization, and the university sets up administrative access.

2

Profile Building

Graduates showcase their skills and experience, while companies highlight their culture and available opportunities.

3

Connection

Companies discover and connect with qualified graduates, while graduates apply for relevant positions and training programs.

4

Engagement

Interviews are scheduled, skills assessments are conducted, and recruitment events are organized.

5

Hiring & Tracking

Successful candidates are hired, and the university tracks graduate employment outcomes for institutional improvement.

6

Growth & Development

Ongoing professional development opportunities are provided, and career progression is monitored for long-term success.

Our Mission

To create a seamless bridge between Beni Suef Technological University graduates and the technology industry by providing a comprehensive platform that facilitates meaningful connections, enhances employability, and tracks career progression.

We strive to empower graduates with opportunities that match their skills and aspirations while enabling companies to discover exceptional talent that drives innovation and growth.

Our Vision

To become the leading Graduate Tracking System in Egypt, setting the standard for university-industry collaboration and serving as a model for other institutions seeking to enhance graduate outcomes.

We envision a future where every Beni Suef Technological University graduate achieves their full career potential, contributing significantly to Egypt's technological advancement and economic development.
